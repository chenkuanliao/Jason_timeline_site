Introduction For This Project

I will use the significant information security incidents in Taiwan in the past two years to create a timeline and introduce Taiwan's recently established Information Security Agency of the Ministry of Digital Development under the Executive Yuan, which mechanism is used to protect the information security of government agencies.

About the Administration for Cyber Security (MOCA)

The History of Administration: The Executive Yuan established the Information Communication Security Office on March 8, 2012, and the Information Communication Security Office was formally established on August 1, 2015. On June 6, 2017, the Information Communication Security Management Law was announced to unify the national information communication security mechanism. The country's overall information security work is officially legalized. On January 19, 2011, the Presidential Decree announced the Organizational Law of the Ministry of Digital Development. The Ministry of Digital Development is responsible for planning, reviewing, and supervising national information security policies, implementing national information security protection, drills and auditing operations, and communication and dissemination foundations. Facility protection, ad hoc information security department Organizational planning: There are five groups and four offices under the agency, which are comprehensive planning, notification of emergency response, counseling and training, audit and inspection, regulations and national cooperation group, handling national information security policy planning, plan review, supervision and assessment, implementation of national Information security protection, drill and audit business, and communication infrastructure protection. The Act applies to: In Taiwan, we have an information security protection law for information security protection, which classifies the information security levels of various agencies in our country and adopts different inspection standards, which are listed in the law. The requirements for communication protection are divided into three levels: high, medium, and low, respectively, in terms of confidentiality, integrity, usability, and compliance with laws and regulations. The impact of the disclosure of the information on the organization's operation is used as the judgment standard, and there are rules for information security details. There are complete and complete regulations, and there are blank clauses to take measures to make up for emergencies. Timeline

  • Chunghwa Telecom Personal Data Leakage Incident

    Chunghwa Telecom's data breach occurred in March 2021, involving the personal data of approximately 3.1 million Chunghwa Telecom customers. According to the investigation, the incident was caused by data leakage due to the failure of the contractor hired by Chunghwa Telecom to keep the data correctly. The leaked information included sensitive personal information such as customer names, phone numbers, addresses, birthdays, and ID numbers. Chunghwa Telecom quickly took measures after the incident, stopped the cooperating contractors and notified the relevant government agencies, and activated the relevant response mechanism to provide free inquiry and monitoring services for the leaked data to protect the rights and interests of customers. This incident caused heavy losses to Chunghwa Telecom and its customers. Also, it aroused social concern about information security, reminding companies and individuals of the importance of protecting their information security.

  • Mackay Hospital Ransomware Attack

    The Mackay Hospital ransomware attack occurred in May 2021. It was a ransomware attack. Attackers used malware to enter the hospital's information system, encrypt the information, and demand payment in Bitcoin. At that time, the patient information system of Mackay Hospital was attacked, and the patient information could not be queried. The doctor's order and medical records could not be added, which affected the patient's medical treatment and care arrangements. The hospital immediately activated the response mechanism, suspended the information system and network connection, and reported the incident to the government unit. Mackay Hospital announced that it would not pay the ransom but would use backup data to restore the system, update and strengthen protection measures for all affected systems, and strengthen information security monitoring and training. According to reports, the attack may have been launched by foreign hackers and has affected medical institutions around the world

  • Cyber attacks on U.S. Navy installations

    The U.S. Navy facility attack occurred in July 2021. A Taiwanese cyber security company discovered that it was an information security attack launched by external hackers, targeting classified U.S. Navy facilities. Specifically, the attackers compromised the network systems at Ballard Naval Base in Washington state and several other naval facilities, including Pacific Fleet Command. The attack lasted several weeks, and the Navy discovered the attack in mid-July. According to reports, the attackers used a unique hacking tool called "GoldMax," developed by a foreign hacker group identified by the U.S. government. Transnational information security incidents have shown the extent of global information security risks. Commonality and globalization, information security incidents between countries and regions often affect and implicate each other. Therefore, strengthening international information security cooperation and information sharing and jointly responding to information security threats and challenges is of great significance to safeguarding global information security and stability.

  • Endpoint network technology company was hacked

    In September 2021, the endpoint network of a Taiwanese information security company was attacked by hackers, and the personal data of about 2 million customers were leaked, including names, ID numbers, birthdays, addresses, and other information. Endpoint Networks is a company that provides cybersecurity services to a wide range of clients, including government agencies and large enterprises. According to reports, the attackers used malware called "Karma" to hack into the information system of the endpoint network and steal a large number of sensitive data of customers.

  • EVA Air Information Security Vulnerability Incident

    The EVA Air Information Security Vulnerability incident occurred in February 2022—a severe information security incident involving EVA Air's website and mobile applications. According to reports, attackers successfully exploited an unpatched vulnerability to compromise EVA Air's website and mobile applications. As a result, they stole many customers' data, including sensitive information such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, birthdays, and passport numbers. In addition, the attackers also stole EVA Air's confidential business information, including flight information, financial records, and customer orders. EVA Air issued an announcement confirming the incident, apologizing to customers and providing relevant assistance and support.

  • Brokerage and Futures Merchant’s ordering system is abnormal due to network service disconnection of telecom operators

    From 9:00 am to 10:30 am on June 28, 2022, many securities dealers' trading systems were disconnected, preventing investors from connecting to the trading systems to place orders. The automatic protection mechanism adopted in the case of abnormality, the occurrence of network abnormality is caused by the number of BGP routes exceeding the upper limit. This measure is to avoid network attacks, but it also affects the rights and interests of many network users. In the future, Chunghwa Telecom will cooperate with Other operators to coordinate reasonable network protection mechanism judgments and increase mechanisms such as horizontal contact and re-confirmation to avoid damage to the rights and interests of the public.s

  • During the US House of Representatives Speaker Filosi’s visit to Taiwan, most of the cyber attacks were web page falsification and distributed denial of service attacks

    During the visit of the Speaker of the U.S. House of Representatives to Taiwan, relevant departments in Taiwan were subjected to a large number of cyber attacks, including a large number of DDOS attacks, dissemination of false information, replacement of supermarket advertisements, attacks on hospitals, infrastructure network systems of transportation facilities, Ministry of Defense, Ministry of Foreign Affairs and various government departments. This incident also indirectly highlights that future cross-strait conflicts are not limited to real life but are moving towards information warfare, which makes our government more aware of the importance of information security.

    A system abnormality occurred when Cathay United Bank upgraded the EFT system and database operations. In the past six months, the ATM crashed four times, which affected the stability of customers' financial services and lacked a proper emergency response mechanism.

    Lion Travel has become a victim of a cyber hacker attack. Criminals fake their names and use order information within half a year to commit fraud. The company's technical experts jointly dealt with it and notified the government law enforcement department for assistance
